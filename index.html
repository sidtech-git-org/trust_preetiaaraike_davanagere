About Our Trust

In loving memory of the late Mrs. Preeti Ravikumar, the Preeti Aaraike Trust was established on April 28, 2023. The trust was founded with heartfelt intentions to continue her legacy of compassion, care, and service to society.

Dr. Ravikumar T G

Founder & Medical Director

Aaraike Super Speciality Hospital, Davangere

Caring for our Community, with accountability and heart

Aaraike Super Speciality Hospital was started with one simple promise: every patient who walks in should receive the same dignity, clarity, and timely treatment that we expect for our own families.

Our consultants, nurses, and emergency team work together 24×7 to stabilise, treat and guide — not just medicate and discharge. We follow safe, NABH-aligned clinical practices, maintain transparent communication with attenders, and keep most diagnostics in-house so patients don’t run around.

We are proud to serve Davangere and surrounding taluks with intensive care, women & child health, trauma support, and preventive medicine — all under one roof.

Our Vision

To create a society where every individual lives with dignity, access to healthcare, and educational opportunity.

Our Mission

To implement sustainable programs in healthcare, food security, rural upliftment, and youth empowerment.
